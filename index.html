Spacegame is a sandbox sci-fi MMO where you build your space ship from scratch with modular pieces that all serve a strategic purpose, command it in tactical battles involving any number of ships, and forge alliances to control sectors and resources. Players can mine for resources, build equipment, and trade. The economy is player-driven, although there are NPC corporations that mine, build, and sell. Players strive for better equipment, experiment with ship builds, and strive for control. Eventually, though, the gods craft their own fleet of battleships and reclaim the universe. The gods reward those who survive the longest with rare augments and equipment when the universe reboots, though.
