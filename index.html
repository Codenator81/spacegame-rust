Spacegame is a sandbox sci-fi MMO. Build and command ships, battle for control of the known universe.

Construct

Forge your space ship from scratch with modular pieces that all serve a strategic purpose. Experiment with ship builds. Mine for resources, build equipment and trade.

Command

Command your vessel in tactical battles involving any number of ships. Confrontations are turn-based, fast-paced, and favor the bold.

Explore

Forge alliances in a sandbox universe. Mine for resources (or just let the corps do it for you). Strive for control. Try to outlast the others when the gods reclaim the universe.
